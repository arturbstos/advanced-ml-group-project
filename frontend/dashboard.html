// privacy_policy.md

            
Privacy Policy

            
Last updated · 2026-04-12 · v1.3


            

                

                    
1 · TL;DR

                    
Your contract is sent to OpenAI for analysis. We do not store the file. We store metadata about the analysis (filename, timestamp, risk counts) so you can find it again on your dashboard. We never sell your data.

                

                

                    
2 · Who we are

                    
veritas is a research project of Nova School of Business and Economics, Lisbon. Data controller under Art. 4 GDPR: veritas-team@novasbe.pt.

                

                

                    
3 · What we collect

                    
    
                        
  • Account data — email, hashed password (bcrypt), language preference.
    • 
                        
  • Analysis metadata — original filename, file size, timestamp, count of HIGH/MED/LOW findings.
    • 
                        
  • Usage — aggregate page views via Plausible (EU-hosted, no IP storage, no cookies).
    • 
                        
  • We do not collect — the contract PDF itself, billing info (Stripe holds that), or location data.
    • 
                    

                

                

                    
4 · Where your contract goes

                    
When you click "Analyze," the PDF is uploaded to our backend on Google Cloud Run (Frankfurt, europe-west1), parsed in memory, and the extracted text is sent to OpenAI's API for clause structuring and embedding.

                    
OpenAI does not train on API data per their Enterprise Data Promise. Embeddings, not the raw text, are stored in our Firestore vector index.

                

                

                    
5 · How long we keep data

                    
    
                        
  • Account: until you delete it (Settings → Danger Zone).
    • 
                        
  • Analysis metadata: 12 months, then auto-purged.
    • 
                        
  • Server logs: 14 days.
    • 
                        
  • Original PDF text: never written to disk.
    • 
                    

                

                

                    
6 · Your rights under GDPR

                    
Articles 15–22 GDPR apply: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection. Email veritas-team@novasbe.pt and we'll respond within 30 days.
